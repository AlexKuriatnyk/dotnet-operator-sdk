Warnings

Despite being "valid", the validation can add a list of warnings to the user. If this is not yet supported by the cluster, the field is ignored. Warnings may contain up to 256 characters but they should be limited to 120 characters. If more than 4096 characters are submitted, additional messages are ignored.
